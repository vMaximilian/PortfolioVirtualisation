Success story
Jushuo Technology

Jushuo Technology assists customers with one-time digital transformation services; in order to promote quality optimization and service efficiency, it is decided to import VMware Tanzu. Now, new function development can be completed within a week, and bug modifications can be completed within one day and the version can be automatically released online. If you build a native K8s environment by yourself, from creating a VM, installing K8s-related packages to creating a K8s cluster, you can now complete the job in 5~10 minutes through Tanzu. Tanzu supports the node self-healing function. Even if the Master or Worker Node is damaged, it will immediately self-heal, and reproduce the same environment and data before the damage without human intervention.
