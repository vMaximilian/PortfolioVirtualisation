Pain point and how we solve it
Pain point

IT teams struggling to manage a growing digital workforce. Companies looking to offer a more flexible work environment but only has traditional tools.Operational, storage and time costs can scale when deploying virtual desktops. External employees need to access ressources similarly as an internal employee without compromising security.

Solution

Stateless desktops, painless application packaging and installation. Improve ROI by securing data, simplifying compliance and dynamically allocate virtual ressources with a single platform that will drive down costs.
